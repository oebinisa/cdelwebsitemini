Programmes We Offer

MBAAccounting

In this program, learners are introduced to, and trained in management and major theories of practices of accounting, thereby preparing them to become certified public accountants (CPAs) and managers in the field.

Full Time 18 months

MBABanking & Finance

An MBA in Banking & Finance trains leaners in banking systems and regulations, financial markets, investing, etc., preparing them for leadership roles in financial corporations, healthcare industries and government.

Full Time 18 months

MBAManagement

Focusing on the importance of organizational & leadership skills, learners will be able to use decision-making and problem-solving skills to enhance organizational growth and increase employee motivation.

Full Time 18 months

MBAMarketing

In this program, aspiring entrepreneurs who are particularly interested in marketing will learn advanced topics in advertising and consumer behavior, international marketing and market research theories.

Full Time 18 months
