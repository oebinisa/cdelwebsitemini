About the MBA Programme

A master’s degree helps you gain specialized knowledge needed to get ahead and transition into more senior positions. The Distance Learning MBA from UNN is the same degree as the one offered on the campus but is designed to allow for flexible learning to deliver the tradition of academic excellence synonymous with UNN.


Interactive Online Courses

Interactive coursework made up of class learning materials, notes, articles and videos, class discussion platforms, private lecturer interfacing and spaced learning.

Flexible Learning Style

Coursework is broken down into weekly topics and deliverables, helping you to better manage your schedule and devote specific hours weekly to allow you participate, earn and deliver on assignments and tasks at your convenience.

Student Management System

Learn alongside others, share ideas and perspectives, discuss course materials and connect with other professionals with diverse levels of experience.

Distance Learning Academic Excellence

About UNN CDeL

The Centre for Distance and e-learning (CDeL) is a response by UNN to the global demand for flexible and broader learning access. The off-campus learning style is built to cater to individuals seeking a flexible system of learning that maintains the same rigorous learning experience and delivers; the same excellence as campus learning and the equally prestigious UNN degree.
